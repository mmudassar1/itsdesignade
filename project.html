Aesop Minimal
Campaign 2024

Client

Aesop Skincare

Services

Strategy, Identity, Web Design, Art Direction

Year

2024

The Challenge

Aesop approached us to reimagine their digital presence for a new minimalist product line. They wanted an immersive experience that mirrored the tactile quality of their packaging and the calming nature of their in-store environments.

The Solution

We designed a web experience that focuses on negative space, slow interactions, and high-fidelity texture photography. The typography is understated yet confident, allowing the product imagery to take center stage. The result is a digital sanctuary that invites users to pause and explore.
